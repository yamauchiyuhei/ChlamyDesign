More candidates + higher temperature = more diverse options. Best GC% (~34%) is auto-selected.

CAI-max: always selects the most frequent codon per amino acid (~29% GC, maximum CSI).
BFC: samples codons proportionally to their frequency, matching natural GC% (~33%).
Base CT: CodonTransformer model (164 organisms) with match_protein guarantee.
ChlamyCT: fine-tuned for chloroplast expression (requires API).
